How to Buy

Floki One

Download MetaMask

You should also be able to purchase $FLOKI via Trust Wallet; however, we personally recommend MetaMask. Please refer to this guide to learn how to install MetaMask. Also, please keep your seed phrase a secret! Never share it with anyone and store it properly!

Add funds

Purchase $ONE from KuCoin (or Binance) and send to your Metamask. You will need to make sure you are using your Harmony ONE mainnet address and not your smart chain address. You can see your $ONE address by entering your smart chain address on the Harmony ONE explorer. Conversely, you can bridge from $BSC to $ONE using the Horizon bridge.

Trade

If you scroll up, you'll come caross a button that is labeled "Buy now on ViperSwap!". Clicking that will redirect you to ViperSwap and with Floki One automatically loaded. You may also click here to buy the token. If by chance you are having trouble loading Floki One on Viper, please manually add the contract address.
